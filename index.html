    Selection of team captains and auction money

    Eight players with the highest trophies from the registration are selected as captains. After the selection of captains, a solo showdown is played between captains and corresponding ranks are noted. Each captain should take a screenshot of their rank after the match to avoid disputes.

      Auction money = 2*(Number of trophies during registration)+1000*(11-rank in solo showdown)

      As an example: if captain A has 15000 trophies during registration, and got 2nd rank in showdown. Then he will have 2*15000+1000*(11-2) = 39000 units of auction money.
