  • Rules

    Coz we are not savages !


    a - Participation Requirements

    Any players violating the rules will loose all the earned points and will be disqualified immediately from the tournament.

      1. Player must be a member of kerala assasins (#20JL0U9Q) brawl stars.

      2. All player should have at least 8000 trophies or more.

      3. All players must be 13 years of age or older on the day of registration.

      4. All players must use their own account. Account sharing is not allowed.

      5. Multiple accounts of the same person are not allowed.

      6. Players may not change accounts/account names once they’ve started their participation.

    b1 - Team formation and rules

      1. A team must contain 3-5 players, but a maximum of 4 players are allowed in the active lineup.

      2. Teams are formed based on auction. Check auction rules for more information.

      3. All players will receive 6 tokens which can be used to play league matches. A player cannot play a league game without their tokens.

      4. Each team should contain:

      a. Team Captain

      b. Team Name

      c. Team Color

      5. Every team member should change their name to team color until the end of the tournament starting from team formation.





    b2 - team captains and auction money

    Eight players with the highest trophies from the registration are selected as team captains. After the selection of captains, a solo showdown is played between captains and corresponding ranks are noted. Each captain should take a screenshot of their rank after the match to avoid disputes.

      Auction money = 30k+0.25*(Number of trophies during registration)+100*(11-rank in solo showdown)

    c1 - PRE LEGUE AUCTION

    There will be two auctions. Before and after the league match. Team captains will be selected and each will be assigned a random number(from 0 to 7) prior to auction. This number will be later mentioned as key value.

      1. Only team captains can participate in the auction.

      2. Base value of players for buying are half the number of trophies during registration.

      3. Bidding of each player starts from his/her base value

      4. Winner will be selected based on the highest bid and bidding amount is deducted from the winner.

      5. Any captain which fails to build a team of 3 or more will be disqualified immediately





    c2 - First bidder

      1. First bidding is forced, on base value.

      2. First bidder is calculated by keyvalue. if '(serial number of player)%8' is matching to assigned keyvalue, then he(team captain) must bid first.

      For example, captain X has a keyvalue of 2, then he is forced to bid first, for the players having registration numbers 10,18,26...etc since 10%8=2. (Note: % is used to find the reminder during a division).

      3. The bid must be greater than or equal to the base value of the player.

      4. Captains other than first bidder can raise the bid if required, else the player will go to the first bidder.

    d1 - LEAGUE MATCH

    Each player will receive six tokens prior to the league match. Players needs to use his tokens to play a league match. Token system maximises the team participation and allows some tactical thinking to gain points.

      1. League matches will contain all the game modes of brawl stars including gem grab(GG), showdown(SD), heist(HS), bounty(BT), brawl ball(BB) and siege(SG).

      2. Each team will play seven matches.

      3. All the maps are selected by the organizers and will be notified to team captains 2 days before the match, except for the home match.

      4. Team lineup including players and brawler selection should be announced one hour before match.

      5. All team members must be present online, 15 minutes before the match.

      6. Each team can ban a brawler during league match, which cannot be taken by both teams.





    d2 - Point distribution

      1. Each win will reward your team 2 points and 0 points for a loss (except for the home match and showdown).

      2. Incase of a tie, 1 point will be awarded to both teams.

      3. Negative points will be awarded for breaking rules such as:.

      - Failure to announce lineup before match (-3)

      - Failure to take screenshot for showdown matches. (-5)

      - Failure to report(be online) 15 minutes before match.(-5)

      - Any other malpractices or violation

    d3 - Home Match

    Home match favours home team.

      1. Any game mode or game map can be selected for home match by the home team except showdown.

      2. The map selection will only be revealed by the home team at the time of the match.

      3. Lineup should be announced one hour before the match.

      4. Home team will receive 1 point for a win in their home match. If the home team loses, the away team will be granted 3 points.

      5. A tie gives 1 point to home team and 2 points to away team.





    d4 - Showdown

      1. A maximum of four players are allowed per team in solo showdown. Vacant positions will be replaced by bots.

      2. Last team to survive wins the match. All players of the team are not required to survive. Even a last man standing can reward points to the team.

      3. Point distribution after eliminating the opponent team.

      - more than 2 players survived : 3 points

      - 2 players survived : 2 points

      - 1 player survived : 1 points

      - 0 players survived : 0 points

      4. Each player requires to take a screenshot of their rank in case of dispute. Failure to do so will cause lose of points to the team.

    e - POST LEAGUE AUCTION

      1. Four teams will be finalised after the league match.

      2. Auction money of each captain is increased to '(auction money left + team points*1000)'

      For example, if captain X has 5000 units of auction money left from the previous auction and his/her team has 10 team points. Then present auction money is 5000+10*1000 = 15000 units.

      3. Auction is repeated with eliminated team players. There won’t be a forced bidding.

      4. Captains cannot discard any player from the team. But player exchanges are allowed. Exchanges are possible only if the player is willing to go to the other team.

      5. Base value of each player increased to ‘number of trophies during registration'.

      6. A team, already having 5 members are not allowed to participate in auction.

      7. Captains can also cash in team points. Each team point will add additional 1000 units to auction money, But this process deducts team points and may affect the league table. Hence, a team should retain minimum points to qualify.





    f - KNOCKOUT

      1. Knockout stage contains 3 rounds

      - Gem grab

      - Bounty

      - Brawll Ball

      2. All the maps will be notified 1 day prior to the match.

      3. Teams with maximum points will continue to finals.

      4. Showdown Solo will be used as a tie breaker incase of a tie.

    g - finals

    Finals will be a surprise round. Finals can be any game mode or any map. Winner of the finals will be decided as the winner of the tournament.

    Prizes worth 2k!!!
